## 🧠 About Me

I am **SV**, founder of **Call O Buzz Services**.

I have been building, fixing, managing, and improving software systems for **15+ years**.

My journey started from older business software stacks like **VB.NET, PHP, classic web applications, shared hosting, and server-side systems**, and over time moved into **modern full-stack development, cloud platforms, AI agents, automation workflows, SaaS products, VPS deployments, and production operations**.

I do not position myself as a “master of one stack”.

My real strength is different:

I focus on solving those problems with the right architecture, the right process, and the right execution.

## 🏗️ How I Think About Technology

I have worked with enough technologies to know one thing clearly:

> The stack is only useful when it solves the problem cleanly.

Some projects need a simple PHP backend.  
Some need a modern Next.js app.  
Some need Supabase.  
Some need a VPS with Docker and Nginx.  
Some need AI agents and automation.  
Some just need better process design.

The job is not to force one stack everywhere.

The job is to understand the goal, constraints, budget, timeline, team, and long-term maintenance — then choose the right path.
